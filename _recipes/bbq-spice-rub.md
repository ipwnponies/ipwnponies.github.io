# Recipe

Ingredient | Weight (g)
-|-
Brown Sugar | 20 g
White Sugar | 20 g
Paprika | 8 g
Garlic Powder | 5 g
Onion Powder | 2 g
Pepper | 2 g
Ginger Powder | 1 g
